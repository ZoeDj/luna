I have the sweetes kitten, named Luna. Luna is eight monts old tabby cat.


Tabby is any domestic cat (Felis catus) that has a coat featuring distinctive stripes, dots, lines or swirling patterns,

always together with a mark resembling an 'M' on its forehead. The tabby pattern is a naturally occurring feature

that may be related to the coloration of the domestic cat's direct ancestor, the African wildcat.
